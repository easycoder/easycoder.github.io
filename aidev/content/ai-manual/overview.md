## One-Page Operating Model

- Start with a human-facing entry page and an agent-facing primer markdown.
- Ship in small steps, with one intent per change.
- Require human understanding before acceptance.
- Guard against stale responses, duplicates, and unclear state transitions.
- Use explicit roles, handoffs, and escalation rules.
- Mature beyond MVP with release discipline and observability.
- Standardize prompting as a team delivery discipline.
